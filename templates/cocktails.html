Flaco Coquito is an organic and vegan Coconut Liqueur: Take Some Inspiration from these cocktails to make your own.

PinaCoquito: 1 part Flaco Coquito, 1 part pineapple Bai beverage, add a pineapple wedge
DairyCoquito:one bottle Flaco Coquito, 1/2 sweetened condensed milk, 1/2 can evaporated milk, dash of cinnamon and nutmeg
CaramelCoquito: 4 oz Flaco Coquito, one tablespoon of caramel syrup, garnish with soft caramel candy
StrawberryCoquito: 1 cup Flaco Coquito, strawberries, 1/2 cup coconut water


BlueHawaiian Coquito: 1 cup Flaco Coquito, blue curacao, pineapple juice
CherryLimeCoquito: 1 cup Flaco Coquito, 2 limes, 1/2 oz grenadine, 1/2 oz simple syrup
MangoOrange: 1/2 cup Flaco Coquito, 1/2 cup coconut water, orange, mango
SunriseCoquito: 1 cup Flaco Coquito, pineapple juice, orange juice, 1/2 cup coconut water
